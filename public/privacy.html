Privacy Policy

Last updated: [Effective Date]

This Privacy Policy explains how [Operator] ("we", "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use Scout, a mobile application for discovering and sharing photography spots (the "App"). By using the App you agree to this Policy.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information. When you sign in we receive your email address and, if available, your display name and profile photo from Firebase Authentication.
  • Profile information. Information you choose to add, such as a profile photo you upload and your home city/country.
  • Content you submit. Photos, ratings, and notes you attach to a photography spot, along with the spot's coordinates and the location details we derive from them (city, region, country).
  • Usage and device data. Standard technical data (such as IP address and app/diagnostic logs) processed by our infrastructure providers to operate and secure the service.

2. Photos and location are public

Spot coordinates and review photos you submit are shared publicly within the App so other users can find and view spots. Do not submit content you want to keep private, and avoid sharing the precise location of sensitive or private places. We remove EXIF metadata (including any embedded GPS data) from uploaded photos before storing them; the spot location is the coordinate you provide, not your device's location.

3. How we use information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the App and its spot-discovery features.
  • To create and manage your account and your submitted content.
  • To reverse-geocode coordinates into place names for display.
  • To protect against fraud, abuse, and security incidents, and to comply with law.

4. Service providers

We rely on third parties to provide the App, including Google Firebase (authentication, database, and file storage) and the Google Maps Platform Geocoding API (converting coordinates to place names). These providers process data on our behalf under their own terms and privacy policies. We do not sell your personal information.

5. Data retention and account deletion

You can delete your account from within the App. When you do, we delete your user profile (including your email, display name, and profile photo) and the underlying authentication record. Reviews and photos you contributed are retained as community content but anonymized — they are detached from your identity rather than removed — so that shared spot information remains useful to others.

6. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You can exercise core rights directly in the App (editing your profile, deleting your account) or by contacting us at [Contact Email].

7. Children

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect their personal information.

8. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, additional notice in the App.

9. Contact

Questions about this Policy? Contact us at [Contact Email].
